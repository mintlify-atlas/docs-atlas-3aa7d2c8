This guide walks you through the complete onboarding flow. Plan around 10–15 minutes to complete account setup and identity verification before you can start tasks.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to the sign-up page and complete the two-step registration form.

    **Step 1 — Security credentials:**
    - Enter your email address (optional — you can register with a username only)
    - Set a login password (minimum 6 characters)
    - Confirm your password
    - Enter an invitation code if you were referred by another agent (optional)

    **Step 2 — Profile details:**
    - Choose a username
    - Enter your phone number
    - Set a 6-digit withdrawal PIN — this is separate from your login password and is required to authorize all withdrawals

    <Warning>
      Your withdrawal PIN cannot be recovered if lost. Store it somewhere secure before completing registration.
    </Warning>

    Once you submit, you are signed in and redirected to your dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify your identity">
    Identity verification is required before your first withdrawal can be approved. From your dashboard, navigate to your account settings and submit your verification documents early to avoid delays later.

    Accepted document types:
    - Government-issued ID card
    - Passport
    - Driver's license

    You will need to upload a photo of the front and back of your document. Your verification status moves through these states: **unverified → pending → verified**. Approval is typically completed within 24 hours.

    <Note>
      If your submission is rejected, you will be notified and can resubmit with a clearer image or a different document type.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Deposit funds to your wallet">
    Your platform wallet holds USDT (Tether). You must deposit funds before you can activate a tier or start tasks.

    From the dashboard, select **Deposit** and follow the instructions to send USDT to your assigned deposit address.

    Key minimums to keep in mind:

    | Purpose | Minimum required |
    |---|---|
    | Start tasks | $65 wallet balance |
    | Junior Agent tier | $100 |
    | Intermediate Agent tier | $500 |
    | Senior Agent tier | $1,500 |
    | Master Agent tier | $5,000 |

    <Tip>
      If you want to activate a tier immediately after depositing, send at least the full tier entry amount. Your wallet balance must cover the tier price to activate it.
    </Tip>

    Deposits are credited to your wallet balance after network confirmation. Check the **Activity records** section to track incoming transactions.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a VIP tier">
    Your tier determines how many tasks you complete per day and the commission rate you earn on each task.

    | Tier | Entry | Commission rate | Daily tasks |
    |---|---|---|---|
    | Junior Agent | $100 | 0.4% | 40 |
    | Intermediate Agent | $500 | 0.6% | 45 |
    | Senior Agent | $1,500 | 0.8% | 50 |
    | Master Agent | $5,000 | 1.0% | 55 |

    Navigate to **VIP Evolution** on your dashboard, select the tier that matches your balance, and confirm. The tier entry amount is deducted from your wallet balance and locked as your optimization capital.

    <Tip>
      The Senior Agent tier is the most popular entry point for agents targeting steady daily returns. You can always upgrade to a higher tier later as your balance grows.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete your first optimization task">
    Once your tier is active and your identity is verified, navigate to **Start tasks** from your dashboard.

    Each task presents a product listing for you to review. After reviewing, submit your rating to complete the task and earn your commission. Repeat until you have completed your full task set for the day.

    Your commission is calculated as:

    ```text
    commission = task value × commission rate
    ```

    For example, a Junior Agent completing a $250 task earns $1.00 (0.4% × $250). Completed tasks accumulate in your **Today's profit** balance in real time.

    <Note>
      Your task set resets each day. Unused tasks from a previous day do not carry over. Complete your full set each day to maximize your daily earnings.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Withdraw your earnings">
    Before your first withdrawal, register your external USDT wallet address in **Account settings**. You will need:
    - Your USDT wallet address
    - The network your wallet uses (e.g., TRC20, ERC20)

    Once your withdrawal wallet is saved, go to **Withdraw** from the dashboard, enter the amount, and confirm using your 6-digit withdrawal PIN.

    Your withdrawal is processed and sent to your registered external wallet. You can track its status in **Activity records**.

    <Tip>
      Keep your registered withdrawal wallet address up to date. Withdrawals are sent to the address on file at the time of the request.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>
