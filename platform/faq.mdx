<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="What is SmartBugMedia and how does it work?">
    SmartBugMedia is a task-based marketplace optimization platform. Agents complete product review and optimization tasks assigned by the platform, earning USDT commissions on each completed task. Tasks are grouped into sets, and agents work through one set at a time. Commissions are credited to your wallet balance as you complete tasks.

    Your earning rate depends on your VIP tier, which is determined by the amount you deposit to activate your account.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I start completing tasks?">
    To begin completing tasks you need:

    1. A verified account with a completed registration.
    2. A wallet balance of at least **$65 USDT** — this is the minimum required to start a task set.
    3. An active VIP tier (activated by making a qualifying deposit).

    Once these conditions are met, navigate to the task center and start your first set. You can complete up to 3 sets per day depending on your tier.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the minimum deposit to start?">
    The minimum deposit to activate a VIP tier and begin tasks is **$100 USDT** (Junior tier). However, you must also maintain a wallet balance of at least **$65 USDT** when starting each new task set.

    Higher tier activations require larger deposits but unlock better commission rates and more tasks per day.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How much can I earn per day?">
    Your daily earnings depend on your VIP tier, your wallet balance, and the number of task sets you complete. Here is an overview of each tier:

    | Tier         | Min. deposit | Commission rate | Tasks/set |
    |--------------|-------------|-----------------|-----------|
    | Junior       | $100        | 0.4%            | 40        |
    | Intermediate | $500        | 0.6%            | 45        |
    | Senior       | $1,500      | 0.8%            | 50        |
    | Master       | $5,000      | 1.0%            | 55        |

    Commission is calculated as a percentage of each task's assigned product value. You can complete up to 3 sets per day, which multiplies your total daily earnings.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is a bundled package task?">
    Bundled package tasks are special high-value orders that can appear at a specific point within your task set. Unlike standard tasks, a bundled task requires additional capital beyond your current wallet balance to complete.

    When a bundled task appears, a modal shows the bundle name, the total amount required, the shortage (how much extra you need to deposit), and the bonus reward you will earn for completing it. If you accept, the total amount is deducted from your wallet and held in your frozen balance until the platform settles the bundle. Your earned bonus is credited when settlement is complete.

    If your wallet balance is insufficient, you must deposit the required shortage amount before accepting the bundle. Declining or not acting on a bundle within the expiry window will clear it from your account.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I withdraw my earnings?">
    To request a withdrawal:

    1. Complete identity verification — your `verification_status` must be `verified`.
    2. Set up your withdrawal wallet address and network in account settings.
    3. Set a withdrawal password and security PIN if you have not already done so.
    4. Go to **Wallet → Withdraw**, enter the amount, confirm your wallet address, and submit.

    Withdrawals are reviewed by an administrator and typically processed within 24 hours. Identity verification is required before your first withdrawal can be approved.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why is my balance frozen?">
    A portion of your balance may be frozen when a bundled package task is assigned mid-set and requires additional funds to complete. The frozen amount represents the difference needed to cover the task.

    Your balance will be unfrozen once you either deposit the required funds and complete the bundled task, or resolve the pending task through customer support. You cannot withdraw a frozen balance until the task is resolved.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does the referral program work?">
    You can invite new users to SmartBugMedia using your unique referral code. When a referred user completes tasks and earns commissions, you receive **20% of their daily earnings** as a referral bonus, credited to your wallet in real time.

    There is no limit to the number of users you can refer. Your referral bonus continues as long as your referred users remain active on the platform.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long do deposits and withdrawals take to process?">
    - **Deposits**: After you submit your deposit and upload the payment proof, an administrator reviews and approves it. Processing typically takes 4–5 hours. Your balance updates as soon as the deposit is approved.
    - **Withdrawals**: Reviewed by an administrator and typically processed within 24 hours of submission.

    You can track the status of any deposit or withdrawal in your transaction history. Contact support if a deposit has not been credited after 24 hours.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What documents are needed for identity verification?">
    Identity verification is required before you can make your first withdrawal. You will need to submit a government-issued photo ID. Accepted document types are:

    - **National ID card** (front and back)
    - **Passport** (photo page)
    - **Driver's license** (front and back)

    Upload clear, unobstructed photos of both sides of the document where applicable. Your verification status will be updated to **pending** after submission. The review process typically takes 1–2 business days.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is a security PIN and when is it used?">
    A security PIN is a numeric code you set in your account settings. It is used as a second layer of identity confirmation for sensitive account actions such as accessing certain account settings or confirming transactions.

    Your security PIN is separate from your login password and your withdrawal password. Keep all three credentials private and do not share them with anyone, including customer support staff.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I have multiple accounts?">
    No. SmartBugMedia enforces a strict **one account per person** policy. Creating or operating multiple accounts is a violation of platform rules and may result in permanent suspension of all associated accounts and forfeiture of balances.

    If you have trouble accessing your account, contact support to recover it rather than registering a new one.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when I complete a full set of tasks?">
    When you complete all tasks in a set, your earned commissions are credited to your available wallet balance. You then have two options:

    - **Withdraw** your available balance.
    - **Start a new set** to continue earning. You can complete up to 3 sets per day; starting a new set resets the task counter and requires the minimum $65 USDT balance to be available.

    Completing multiple sets in a day accumulates more earnings before a withdrawal.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I upgrade my VIP tier?">
    Your VIP tier is determined by your deposit amount. To upgrade, make a deposit that meets or exceeds the minimum deposit threshold for the target tier:

    - **Intermediate**: $500 USDT
    - **Senior**: $1,500 USDT
    - **Master**: $5,000 USDT

    Contact customer support to apply for a tier upgrade after your deposit is confirmed. Upgrading your tier increases your commission rate and the number of tasks available per set.
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
