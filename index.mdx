## How SmartBugMedia works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at SmartBugMedia and complete your profile, including phone number and identity verification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fund your wallet">
    Deposit USDT into your agent wallet to activate your tier and begin completing tasks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a VIP tier">
    Choose a tier level that matches your investment. Higher tiers offer more tasks per day and higher commission rates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete daily tasks">
    Use the Optimization Command Center to match and complete product review tasks each day and earn commission rewards.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grow your network">
    Invite others using your referral code to earn a 20% bonus on their daily optimization yields.
  </Step>
</Steps>
