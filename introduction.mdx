SmartBugMedia connects a global network of agents with decentralized marketplaces that need product validation and optimization. As an agent, you complete daily review tasks assigned to your tier, earn a commission on each task, and withdraw your profits to your own USDT wallet. The platform handles task assignment, commission calculation, and transaction processing automatically.

Your earning potential scales with the tier you choose. Higher tiers offer more tasks per day, higher commission rates, and access to premium optimization cycles. Every agent also receives a unique referral code — when someone you refer completes tasks, you earn 20% of their daily profits as a referral bonus on top of your own commissions.

<Note>
  SmartBugMedia uses a task-set model. Each day you receive a set of product review tasks to complete. You earn a commission percentage of the task value on every completed task. Your wallet balance, today's profit, and referral earnings are all tracked separately in your dashboard.
</Note>

## How the platform works

SmartBugMedia operates in three stages:

1. **Fund your wallet** — Deposit USDT to your platform wallet. A minimum balance of $65 is required before you can start tasks. To activate a tier, you need the full tier entry amount available.
2. **Select a tier** — Purchase a VIP tier level that matches your investment. Your tier determines your daily task count and commission rate.
3. **Complete tasks and earn** — Each day, a new set of product review tasks is assigned to your account. Complete them to accumulate commission. At the end of each cycle, your earnings are credited to your wallet balance and available for withdrawal.
