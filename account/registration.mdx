Creating an account on SmartBugMedia takes a few minutes. The sign-up flow is split into two steps: setting your credentials, then completing your profile. After registration you will need to fund your wallet and select a VIP tier before you can start tasks.

## Sign-up flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the sign-up page">
    Navigate to the SmartBugMedia sign-up page. If someone invited you, use their referral link — it will pre-fill your invitation code automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    Provide the following information in step one of the form:

    - **Email address** — optional, but required if you want email-based login and recovery.
    - **Password** — at least 6 characters.
    - **Confirm password** — must match exactly.
    - **Invitation code** — the 4-character code from the agent who referred you (optional). If you received a referral link, this field is pre-filled.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete your profile">
    Step two collects your identity information:

    - **Username** — your unique handle on the platform.
    - **Phone number** — required for account identification.
    - **Withdrawal PIN** — a 6-digit numeric PIN used to authorize all withdrawals. Set this carefully; you will need it every time you withdraw funds.
    - **Confirm withdrawal PIN** — must match your chosen PIN exactly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your registration">
    Click **Complete Setup**. The platform creates your account and sends a welcome email if you provided an email address.

    - **With email:** you are redirected to a success page. Check your inbox for a confirmation link and click it to verify your email address before logging in.
    - **Without email:** you are signed in automatically and redirected to the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fund your wallet and select a tier">
    A new account starts with a zero balance. You must deposit USDT and activate a VIP tier before the Optimization Command Center becomes available. See [Deposits](/transactions/deposits) and [VIP tiers](/features/tiers) for next steps.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your invitation code is the referral code of the agent who invited you — also called their Node ID. Entering it correctly links your account to their network so they receive referral earnings from your activity.
</Note>

## Email verification

If you registered with an email address, SmartBugMedia sends a confirmation link to that address. Click the link to verify your email. Until verification is complete, some account features may be restricted.

If you did not receive the email, check your spam folder. You can also resend the confirmation from your profile settings at any time.

## Identity verification

Identity verification is required before you can make your first withdrawal. It is not required to complete tasks or deposit funds.

### Verification states

Your account has a `verification_status` that progresses through the following states:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Unverified** | No documents have been submitted yet. |
| **Pending** | Documents submitted and under review. Typically reviewed within 24–48 hours. |
| **Verified** | Identity confirmed. Withdrawals are fully unlocked. |
| **Rejected** | Documents were not accepted. You can resubmit with corrected or clearer documents. |

### Accepted document types

SmartBugMedia accepts the following government-issued documents:

- **Government ID** — national identity card (front and back).
- **Passport** — photo page (front only required; back upload is optional).
- **Driver's license** — front and back.

### How to submit your documents

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your profile">
    Open your profile and navigate to the identity verification section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your document type">
    Choose government ID, passport, or driver's license from the document type selector.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload front and back images">
    Upload a clear, well-lit photo of the front of your document. Upload the back image where required. Make sure all text and the photo on the document are fully visible — blurry or cropped images will be rejected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit for review">
    Submit your documents. Your `verification_status` changes to **Pending**. You will be notified once the review is complete.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Do not submit documents belonging to another person. Submitting falsified identity documents will result in permanent account suspension.
</Warning>
