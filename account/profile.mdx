Your profile is the central place to manage your account information. It also displays your wallet balances, referral earnings, and links to all account management sections.

## Profile overview

When you open your profile, you see three key balance figures at the top:

- **Available balance** — your current wallet balance, ready to withdraw or use.
- **Daily profits** — commission earned from today's completed optimization tasks.
- **Referral bonus** — total earnings from your referred agents' daily profits.

Below the balances, your profile is divided into two navigation groups: **Protocol Logs** (task history, deposit records, withdrawal records) and **Identity Shard Control** (security, wallet, email, settings).

### Avatar

Your profile avatar appears in the dashboard header. To update it:

1. Open your profile page.
2. Click your avatar — a camera overlay appears.
3. Select an image file from your device.
4. The image uploads automatically and your avatar updates.

Accepted formats are standard image types (JPG, PNG, WebP). The image is cropped to a square.

## Language and currency

You can customize how the platform displays text and financial values.

**Language** — controls the interface language. Available options include English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, Arabic, Portuguese, Turkish, Russian, Hindi, and Vietnamese. Changes apply immediately.

**Currency** — controls how balances and earnings are displayed. The platform supports over 20 currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, AED, INR, and others. This is a display preference only; all actual transactions are settled in USDT.

To change either setting, go to **Profile → Settings** and tap the language or currency row.

## Your referral code (Node ID)

Every agent has a unique referral code that also serves as their Node ID. You can find yours in two places:

- **Dashboard header** — the Node ID badge is shown next to your username at the top of the home screen. Click it to copy the code to your clipboard.
- **Profile page** — the **Secure Access Token** widget under your username shows your referral code. Click it to copy.

Share your Node ID with others to invite new agents. Anyone who registers using your referral code is linked to your network and you earn a 20% bonus on their daily profits. See [Referral program](/account/referrals) for details.

## Notification preferences

The **Notifications** toggle in **Profile → Settings** controls whether the platform sends push notifications to your device. You can enable or disable this at any time; the setting is saved to your account and syncs across sessions.

## Profile sections

| Section | Path | What you can do |
|---|---|---|
| My Information | Profile → My Information | View username, email, phone, referral code, join date |
| Email | Profile → Email | Bind or update email address |
| Phone | Profile → Phone | Add or update phone number |
| Security | Profile → Security | Set or update your 6-digit security PIN |
| Wallet | Profile → Wallet | Add or update your withdrawal wallet address |
| Settings | Profile → Settings | Language, currency, notifications, password, theme |
