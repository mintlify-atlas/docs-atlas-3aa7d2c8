Complete account setup is required before you can start tasks and withdraw earnings. This page covers each configuration step in detail.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have the following ready:

- **A USDT wallet** — You need an external USDT wallet to receive withdrawals. Note the wallet address and the network it uses (e.g., TRC20, ERC20, BEP20).
- **Identity documents** — One of the following: a government-issued ID card, a passport, or a driver's license. You will upload photos of both the front and back.
- **A phone number** — Required during registration.

## Account registration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the sign-up page">
    Navigate to the SmartBugMedia sign-up page. If you received an invitation from another agent, locate their referral code before you begin — you can enter it during registration but not after.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Step 1: Security credentials">
    Fill in the security credentials form:

    - **Email address** — Optional. If you leave this blank, your account will use a username-based login.
    - **Password** — Must be at least 6 characters. This is your login password.
    - **Confirm password** — Re-enter your password to confirm.
    - **Invitation code** — Optional. Enter the 4-character referral code from the agent who invited you.

    Click **Continue to profile** to proceed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Step 2: Profile details">
    Fill in your profile information:

    - **Username** — Your display name on the platform. Choose carefully; this becomes your agent identity.
    - **Phone number** — Enter your full phone number including country code.
    - **Withdrawal PIN** — A 6-digit PIN used to authorize all withdrawal requests. This is separate from your login password.
    - **Confirm withdrawal PIN** — Re-enter the PIN to confirm.

    <Warning>
      Your withdrawal PIN is the only authorization required to move funds out of your account. Do not share it with anyone. There is no recovery option if you lose it.
    </Warning>

    Click **Complete setup** to create your account. You will be signed in automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Identity verification

Identity verification is required before you can make your first withdrawal. Submit your documents early to avoid delays when you are ready to withdraw your earnings.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to verification settings">
    From your dashboard, go to your account or profile settings and locate the identity verification section. Your current verification status is displayed there.

    Verification states:

    | Status | Meaning |
    |---|---|
    | `unverified` | No documents submitted yet |
    | `pending` | Documents submitted and under review |
    | `verified` | Approved — you can start tasks |
    | `rejected` | Submission declined — resubmit required |
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your document type">
    Select one of the accepted document types:

    - **ID card** — Government-issued national identity card
    - **Passport** — International travel passport
    - **Driver's license** — Valid driving licence with photo

    Choose the document that shows your legal name most clearly.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload document photos">
    Upload a clear photo of:
    - The **front** of your document
    - The **back** of your document

    Photo requirements:
    - All four corners of the document must be visible
    - Text must be fully legible — no glare, blur, or shadows
    - The document must not be expired

    Submit your documents for review.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for approval">
    After submission, your status changes to `pending`. Reviews are typically completed within 24 hours.

    <Note>
      If your submission is rejected, your status returns to `unverified` and you will be notified. Review the rejection reason and resubmit with a clearer photo or a different document type.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Setting up your withdrawal wallet

You must register your external USDT wallet before you can submit a withdrawal request. The platform sends funds to this address when you withdraw.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open wallet settings">
    Go to your account settings and locate the wallet configuration section.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your wallet address">
    Paste your external USDT wallet address into the **Wallet address** field. Double-check the address carefully — withdrawals sent to an incorrect address cannot be reversed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select the wallet network">
    Choose the network your wallet uses from the **Wallet network** dropdown. Common options include TRC20, ERC20, and BEP20. Make sure the network you select matches the network your external wallet supports.

    <Warning>
      Sending USDT on the wrong network will result in permanent loss of funds. Confirm the network with your wallet provider if you are unsure.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save your withdrawal wallet">
    Save your settings. Your registered wallet address is now on file and will be used for all future withdrawal requests.

    <Note>
      You can update your withdrawal wallet address at any time in account settings. Withdrawals are always sent to the address on file at the time of the request.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Security PIN

Your 6-digit withdrawal PIN is set during registration and is required to authorize every withdrawal. To change it:

1. Go to your account settings.
2. Locate the security or PIN section.
3. Enter your current PIN and then set a new one.

<Warning>
  If you forget your withdrawal PIN, contact support through the Concierge Hub on your dashboard. PIN recovery requires identity verification.
</Warning>

## Language and currency preferences

You can configure display preferences in your account settings:

- **Language** — Sets the display language for the platform interface.
- **Currency** — Sets the currency used to display wallet balances, profits, and transaction amounts throughout the dashboard. This is a display setting only; all actual balances and transactions are denominated in USDT.

To update these settings, go to your account settings and update the **Language** and **Currency** fields, then save.

## Pre-task checklist

Before you start your first task set, confirm that all of the following are complete:

- [ ] Account registered with a username and password
- [ ] Withdrawal PIN set and stored securely
- [ ] Identity verification submitted and approved (`verified` status)
- [ ] USDT deposited — at least $65 available balance
- [ ] VIP tier activated
- [ ] Withdrawal wallet address registered with the correct network

Once all items are complete, navigate to **Start tasks** from your dashboard to begin your first optimization cycle.
